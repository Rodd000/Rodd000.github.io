Ever since the pandemic, i've had an interest in playing a popular online gaming platform in which many know as Roblox. The unlimited catalog of experiences and games that are availabe to play is just irresistible. Below here are two of my favorite games and some honorable mentions in Roblox.

Tower Defense Simulator is a strategy game where you place towers to help you defend your base from hordes of various enemies. The models, maps, and everything is designed very well. I also liked their OST which is really upbeat and energetic. I really love playing tower defense games so this was an easy pick for me.

"Les morts marcheront sur la terre." In this world, no one can survive alone. You are grouped with a squad of 19 other people trying to fend off the endless horde of zombies infected with "The Blight". This game is incredibly team-based so every person with their own class/role matter. Sometimes the moments I have with people here are just funny and that I love every experience I have playing this game.

There are games like Arsenal, Islands, and Fisch in which I had a really good time playing with but I decided to put them all in here. Arsenal is a shooter game in which I enjoy, Islands is an MMORPG game inspired heavily from Minecraft Skyblock, and Fisch is a relaxing game where you fish things from a certain rarity.
