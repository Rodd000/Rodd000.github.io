Though I have only watched a handful amount of animes in my life, I still have my favorite. Neon Genesis Evangelion isn't just a mecha anime, it dives more into the psychological well-being of the characters. The anime is cool and the messages that they bring about loving yourself makes this anime my most beloved.

Mysterious beings called Angels started attacking humanity in 2015. In order to combat them, a secret organization called NERV builds robots called Evangelions which were piloted by teens. Their parent organization, SEELE, plans on triggering an event that would merge all human souls into one, eradicating individuality.

It's a 1995 anime created by Hideaki Anno and produced by Gainax. The anime's story mixes the genre of apocalyptic sci-fi with psychological themes of identity, trauma, and the human desire for connection. It is also famous for its complex symbolism, use of religious imagery, and its controversial ending, making it one of the most influential works in anime.
