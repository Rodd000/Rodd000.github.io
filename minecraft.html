Blocks, blocks, blocks, and even more blocks. I love Minecraft like everyone. It's a really fun game with endless possibilities on what you could do.

I like building stuff in Minecraft. It's simple and quite complicated sometimes. You place blocks to make something that you could brag to your friends.

Speaking of friends, why not play with them together? You could build, mine, venture as a team and everyones there for one another. Seriously, it's funny to play with friends because the things that you could do is so much more.

The world is vast and there's tons and tons of places to explore. Caves, cliffs, rivers, valleys, and many more. Spawned on a biome where you don't like? No worries! Just walk 500 miles until you're satisfied with where you place your dirt mansion.
