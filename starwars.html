I love Star Wars ever since I was a little child. I was amazed with everything in it whether it was the setting, the story, or even the duels. This franchise will remain as the best thing that has happened to my life.

Star Wars is a sci-fi movie series about the battle between the Jedi and Sith. It follows the eventful rise, tragic fall, and the redemption of Anakin Skywalker. The franchise, at its core, is all about hope, family, and the struggle between good and evil.

A story by George Lucas that happened a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away where countless planets, species, and cultures exist. The galaxy is torn between the forces of democracy, like the Galactic Republic and later the Rebellion/Resistance. Within the mess is the Force, a mystical energy that binds all life, split between the light side and the dark side.
